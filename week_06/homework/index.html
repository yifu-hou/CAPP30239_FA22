INVESTIGATING POLICE SHOT DEATH DATA

1 - Demographic Distribution of 2015 Police Shot Death

The chart presents the ethnicity distribution of people shot by police. As the chart shows, the majority of the data are identified as white and black. What's worth noticing is that even though the two groups takes up large proportion of police shot death, they are very different when it comes to whether they were armed with a gun or not (if time permits, a horizontal stacked bar chart should better present this).

2 - 2015 Police Shot Death by Time

This line chart shows the number of deaths from police shooting in the year of 2015, with x-axis being the timeline and y-axis represents the number of deaths.

3 - 2015 Police Shot Death Age Distribution

This histogram shows the age distribution of deaths from police shooting in the year of 2015. The whole area represents the total number of people and the height of different bars represent the number of people belonging to that age group.
